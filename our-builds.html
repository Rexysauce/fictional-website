G-Series G30-900 62mm turbo

This popular modular turbo is recommended for race applications only. Due to it's very high compression, and need for a sophisticated oil and cooling system, we recommend spending the extra dime. However once you can feed this monster, it will provide high-RPM performance that is rivaled by almost none.

TMG 2JZ-GTE Camshaft

This high lift camshaft paired with a turbo system is perfect for race applications. While the low RPM suffers from torque dip, the powerband this camshaft provides at the high RPM range is consistent and predictable, letting you approach corners with a much more precise speed input.

These track rated coilovers offer stiff rebound around corners, and help reduce roll to maintain traction. With adjustable spring rates these coils can be used for many motorsport applications from time attack to drifting.
